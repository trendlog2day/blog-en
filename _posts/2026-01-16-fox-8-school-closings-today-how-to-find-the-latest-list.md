## Delays vs. Full Closings

One thing that often confuses people is the difference between a delay and a full closure. When searching for **fox 8 school delays and closings**, pay close attention to the details.

A "Two-Hour Delay" is common when road crews just need a bit of extra time to salt the main arteries. I’ve noticed this happens frequently when the snow stops early in the morning. However, if conditions don't improve by mid-morning, those delays can sometimes turn into cancellations.
